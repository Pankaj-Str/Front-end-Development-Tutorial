The main idea of "The Psychology of Money" is that financial success is more about behavior and mindset than technical knowledge. It explores how emotions, biases, and individual experiences shape our relationship with money and influence our financial decisions.

"The Psychology of Money" is suitable for anyone interested in personal finance, investing, or understanding the psychological aspects of money management. It is particularly beneficial for individuals looking to improve their financial decision-making and develop a healthier relationship with money.
