Green Apples: A Crisp and Refreshing Delight

Green apples, also known as Granny Smith apples, are a popular variety of apple that is known for its tart flavor and crisp texture. Here are some key details about green apples:

  • Appearance: Green apples have a bright green skin that is smooth and shiny. They are typically medium to large in size.
  • Flavor: Green apples have a tart and tangy flavor with a hint of sweetness. They are often used in baking and cooking due to their ability to hold their shape when cooked.
  • Nutritional Value: Green apples are low in calories and high in fiber, making them a healthy snack option. They also contain vitamins A and C, as well as antioxidants.
  • Uses: Green apples can be eaten fresh, used in salads, or cooked in various dishes such as pies, crisps, and sauces. They are also popular for making cider.
