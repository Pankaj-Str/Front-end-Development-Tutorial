Welcome to Joy Samosa Point

A samosa is a popular, crispy, deep-fried South Asian pastry with a savory filling, most commonly spiced potatoes, onions, and peas, though meat or cheese versions exist; it's a beloved street food and tea-time snack, served hot with chutneys like mint or tamarind. Originating from the Middle East, it's a triangular snack with a flaky shell, made from flour and water, known for its satisfying crunch and flavorful spiced interior.
